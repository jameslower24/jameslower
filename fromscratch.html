Places I Lived

  1. Atacames, Esmeraldas
    • A beautiful coastal town
    • Amazing street food: Encebollado, Bolon de Verde, Encocado de Camaron
  2. Ibarra, Imbabura
    • City in the Andes mountains, sits below a massive volcano
    • Famous for colonial architecture
  3. Lago Agrio, Sucumbios
    • Amazon basin frontier town
    • So much humidity, rain, and wildlife
  4. Quito, Pichincha
    • The capital, 9,350 feet above sea level
    • Old Town is a UNESCO World Heritage Site

Old Town Quito: where I finished my mission.
